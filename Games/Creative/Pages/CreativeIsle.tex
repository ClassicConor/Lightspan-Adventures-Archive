\section{Overview}

Creative Isle has a total of five locations to explore within the game:

\begin{itemize}
    \item Beach
    \item Pirate Ship
    \item Cave
    \item Tree house
    \item Forest
\end{itemize}

The characters include:
\begin{itemize}
    \item A giant pink slug
    \item A royal eel
    \item A young child
    \item A teenage woman
\end{itemize}
